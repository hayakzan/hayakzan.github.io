What is hayakzan?
hayakzan stems from <em>Hayy ibn Yaqdhan</em>, (حي بن يقظان, Ar. “Alive, son of Awake”), a philosophical novel by Ibn Tufail, a 12th-century polymath. The book gives the reader a direct "feel" and understanding of the intellectual climate of the Islamic Golden Age. You can read it here:
https://web.archive.org/web/20051210130856/http://umcc.ais.org/~maftab/ip/pdf/bktxt/hayy.pdf
